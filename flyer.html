What is Mutual Aid?
Mutual Aid is the idea that when we work together as a community we all benefit.
Mutual Aid is taking responsibility for everyone through direct action rather than symbolic acts or relying on politicians or the state.

If you're able, fill out and distribute this flyer ( http://BIGOLADDRESS.com ) and create mutual aid groups to support your neighbourhood and communities (especially those who don't have access to Facebook and the groups on there!)
